How is the repository/project organized?
People usually put their best code in the master branch and put the the most important projects on their profile page. Beside, they will use tag to highlight the information/version. 

How do they resolve pull requests?
People first will pull the current code to a branch, and do some bug fix. Second, they will commit the changes and push them to the repository to see if the bug have been fixed.

Can you get a sense of how contributions are evaluated in this community?
By reviewing the stars they have, we could know how popular the repository is. 

How is the community organized?
Github allows people to collaborate across many projects at once. It can freely share resources online and manage members who can assess to the projects.
